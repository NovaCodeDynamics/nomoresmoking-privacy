Privacy Policy

No More Smoking (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect your email address to create and authenticate your account, provide essential features (such as login and password recovery), and respond to support requests.

Health Data

We collect user-provided health-related data such as smoking habits, goals, progress, and milestones. This data is used only to provide core app functionality and progress tracking.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Store your progress and preferences
  • Keep the app secure and reliable
  • Respond to support requests

Tracking & Advertising

We do not use your email address or health data for tracking purposes, and we do not sell your personal data.

Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or tracking. We may use service providers (such as hosting or email delivery) only to operate the app.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

Advertising and Tracking

We use third-party advertising services, such as Google AdMob, to display ads within the app, including rewarded advertisements. These services may collect device identifiers (such as Advertising ID) to provide personalized or non-personalized ads, measure ad performance, and prevent fraud.

Tracking is conducted in accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. Users are asked for permission before any tracking is enabled. If permission is not granted, tracking is disabled and only non-personalized ads may be shown.

We do not collect or use device identifiers to personally identify users. Advertising and tracking data is handled by third-party providers in accordance with their own privacy policies.
